COURSE: PSY 101 – Introduction to Psychology
Professor: Dr. Lauren Richards
Semester: Spring 2025

Course Description:
This course introduces the scientific study of human behavior and mental processes. Topics include biological bases of behavior, cognition, learning, personality, and psychological disorders.

Required Textbook:
Psychology: Mind & Behavior, 6th Edition, by David Cohen
Additional readings will be posted on Canvas.

Recommended Texts:
Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman
